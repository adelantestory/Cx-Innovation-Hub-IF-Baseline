# Stage 1: Customer Discovery

**Phase:** 1 - Strategy Briefing / A Day in the Life  
**Duration:** Part of 1 day (shared with Stages 2-3)  
**Primary Actor:** Human

## Overview

Human performs discovery with the customer. This may involve whiteboarding sessions, architecture design sessions, a day-in-the-life engagement, or simply a business analysis exercise with the customer.

## Process

1. Human conducts discovery sessions with customer
2. As available, human records all interactions and generates transcripts automatically
3. Human designates which speakers are Microsoft staff and which represent the customer
4. All discovery material placed in `artifacts/` folder for agent reference

## Materials to Collect

- Meeting transcripts
- Whiteboard photos/diagrams
- Architecture diagrams
- Process flow documents
- Screenshots of existing systems
- Data samples (sanitized)
- API specifications
- Requirements documents
- Email threads with context

## Agent Involvement

Agents are **not directly involved** in this stage. This is human-to-customer interaction.

## Deliverables

| Deliverable | Location | Owner |
|-------------|----------|-------|
| Transcripts | `artifacts/` | Human |
| Images/Diagrams | `artifacts/` | Human |
| Additional documentation | `artifacts/` | Human |

## Exit Criteria

- [ ] All discovery sessions completed
- [ ] Materials organized in `artifacts/` folder
- [ ] Human ready to engage agents for SOW generation

## Next Stage

Proceed to **Stage 2: Scope of Work Generation** when all discovery materials are collected.
