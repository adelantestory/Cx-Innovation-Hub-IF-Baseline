# Stage 3: Customer Sign-Off and Approval

**Phase:** 1 - Strategy Briefing / A Day in the Life  
**Duration:** Part of 1 day (shared with Stages 1-2)  
**Primary Actor:** Human

## Overview

The scope of work is transferred to a formal Word document and shared with the customer for alignment and approval.

## Process

1. Human transfers `deliverables/SCOPE_OF_WORK.md` to formal Word document
2. Human shares document with customer
3. Customer and human work together to ensure alignment
4. Modifications captured in Word document (markdown SOW remains unchanged)
5. Document finalized upon customer approval
6. Approved Word document stored in `deliverables/` folder

## Naming Convention

Preferred naming: `MM-dd <customer>.docx`

Example: `01-15 Contoso.docx`

## Agent Involvement

**Agents are not directly involved in this stage.** This is human-to-customer negotiation.

However, if significant scope changes are requested:
- Human may re-engage `project-manager` and `business-analyst`
- Changes should be evaluated against 10-day constraint
- Out-of-scope items should be documented for "Phase 2"

## Deliverables

| Deliverable | Location | Owner |
|-------------|----------|-------|
| Formal SOW (Word) | `deliverables/MM-dd <customer>.docx` | Human |

## Exit Criteria

- [ ] Customer has reviewed SOW
- [ ] All concerns addressed
- [ ] Customer has formally approved
- [ ] Approved Word document stored in `deliverables/`
- [ ] Human ready to begin prototyping phase

## Next Stage

Proceed to **Stage 4: Agent Discovery** when customer approval is obtained.
